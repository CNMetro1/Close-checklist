How to Install App

iOS / iPhone

  1. Tap the Share icon (square with arrow)
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap Add to finish

Android / Chrome

  1. Tap the Menu (three dots)
  2. Tap "Install app"
  3. Confirm the installation
